Details

  • Water salinity

  • Select one of the three ways to evaluate the water salinity at the dive location.

    • Seawater (average) Average seawater salinity.

    • Freshwater Average fresh water salinity.

    • Seawater (at location) Allow to select the seawater salinity on a map.

  • Water surface temperature

  • Select the temperature of the water (at the surface) at your dive location.

  • Available only with the Seawater (at location) mode.

  • Map

  • The map displays an colored overlay representing the level of salinity at each location.

    Select (double click) the nearest location to your dive site.

    Click on the Current to center the map at your current location.

  • Month

  • Select the month during which the dive occurs. Changes to the selection will update the salinity overlay on the map.
